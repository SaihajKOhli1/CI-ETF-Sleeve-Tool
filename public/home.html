What Is This Tool?

CI Global Asset Management is a leading Canadian investment firm offering a comprehensive range of public ETFs and private-market solutions designed to help investors build diversified portfolios.

This simulator lets you compare a baseline portfolio against two enhanced scenarios: adding a CI ETF sleeve using real historical pricing, or blending in a Private Markets proxy sleeve modeled on public proxies and assumptions. The goal is to visualize how each adjustment might affect risk, return, and diversification.

This is an educational simulation only—it does not constitute investment advice. No portfolio data is stored on any server; all calculations run locally in your browser.
